Our comprehensive investor profile is based on decades of research in behavioral finance and real-life experience managing investments for high net worth individuals. Each question was carefully evaluated by renowned economist and pioneer in behavioral finance, Professor Hersh Shefrin.

Your Challenge:
 "I have state-of-the-art portfolio reporting but still use paper questionnaires."

Our Solution: Paperless Discovery
Bringing a new client on-board should be efficient and easy for everyone. Our client data intake solution is optimized for the iPad so you never need to take paper forms to a meeting again. You can ask the questions and enter the responses for your client/prospect, or have them respond directly by handing them an iPad/tablet or having them complete the questions on their own time, either before or after your meeting.

Your Challenge:
 "I have multiple questionnaires for everything from KYC and account opening, to risk tolerance, IPS, and client interests. "

Our Solution: Comprehensive Profiles
A centralized place to capture, manage, and view everything you know about a client. Create and manage comprehensive client profiles including personal demographics, investing preferences and restrictions, financial and personal goals, hobbies and interests, assets/income/expenses, risk tolerance, lifestyle, financial behavioral traits and biases all in one easily accessible location.

Your Challenge:
 "I have to fumble through meeting notes and emails every time I need to reference when and why decisions were made."

Our Solution: Profile Audit Tracking
Easily view what, why, and when changes were made to all client data, including goals, needs, preferences, and risk tolerance. Meet compliance requirements with complete audit logging of all profile data changes.
