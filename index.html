Hersh Shefrin
Behavioral Finance Advsor
Hersh Shefrin is a Professor of Finance at Santa Clara University's Leavey School of Business where he holds the Mario Belotti Chair in the Department of Finance. A pioneer of behavioral finance, Professor Shefrin, together with Meir Statman, introduced the concept of “the disposition effect” in 1985, and coined the term. He has authored numerous books including: A Behavioral Approach to Asset Pricing; Beyond Greed and Fear: Understanding Behavioral Finance and the Psychology of Investing; and, Behavioral Corporate Finance. Professor Shefrin completed his PhD at the London School of Economics in the Economics of Uncertainty.
