How does your personality influence how you manage your wealth?

Your financial habits and decisions have as much to do with nature - what’s in your DNA, as nurture – influences from the world around you. Age, gender, disposition, and life experiences can influence your financial decisions for both better and worse. Understanding your investment personality, including how you think and feel about managing your wealth, can help you make better informed decisions.
